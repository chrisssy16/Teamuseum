Tea is one of Sri Lanka’s major export commodities; it sustains the livelihoods of the majority of the estate population in Sri Lanka.Despite the sector’s prominence, however, the benefits have not trickled down to the estate sector population.

Tea pluckers are an essentare a group of people in the tea Industry who are employed at minimum wage, mostly living in shanties closer to the estate they work.For all these years the facilities and living standards of tea pluckers have been miserable and not taken care of by the authorities.

Tea plucking is an authentic profession that requires experience and perseverance as the quality of the tea is mostly remnant on the goodness of the leaves that are pluck. Tea pluckers are a community that adds colour and beauty to the world of tea enriching the handmade goodness of authentic Ceylon tea winning hearts of worldwide tea aficionados.
